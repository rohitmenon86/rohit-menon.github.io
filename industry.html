Professional Background

My professional background has focused on the practical application of AI and robotics in real-world environments. I enjoy the challenge of taking laboratory breakthroughs and scaling them into robust, reliable products.

Founding Engineer & Group Leader (AI in Manipulation) Nov 2019 — Jan 2022
NEURA Robotics, Metzingen, Germany

As the first software engineer in the AI team, I developed the complete software infrastructure for AI-driven robotic applications. Successively promoted to Group Leader for AI for Manipulation in September 2021.

  • Developed deep learning-based pick-and-place systems, enabling selective grasping by fusing semantic segmentation with 3D depth data.
  • Built comprehensive human detection and collision avoidance schemes using multi-sensor fusion for safe human-robot interaction.
  • Conceptualized and developed a showcase robot for human-robot engagement (Tic-Tac-Toe).
  • Architected the software stack using C++ libraries, ROS/Gazebo, and custom device drivers.
  • Featured in international media for demonstrating AI-driven robots at GITEX 2021 in Dubai.
Researcher (Robot Control) July 2015 — Oct 2019
DFKI (Robotics Innovation Center), Bremen, Germany

Focused on motion planning, grasping, and trajectory generation for mobile and humanoid manipulators.

  • Contributed to major EU and industry projects including Hybr-IT, TransFIT, NeTTUN, and iLAADR, focusing on HRI, teleoperation, and sensor-based manipulation.
  • Served as DFKI Project Coordinator for the EIT Digital project iLevator (2016).
  • Developed motion planning and trajectory generation frameworks for mobile manipulators in unstructured environments.
  • Supervised Master's research on multi-sensor fusion for in-hand object localization.
Senior Executive (Engineering & Commissioning) Sept 2008 — Sept 2012
Siemens Ltd, Thane, India

Led large-scale industrial automation projects, focusing on drive control schemes and commissioning of complex machinery.

  • Developed AC/DC drive control schemes, PLC programming, and HMI designs for industrial applications.
  • Optimized machine logic and tuned control loops during commissioning.
  • Managed technical interfaces between clients, consultants, and vendors.
  • Promoted through roles from Graduate Trainee Engineer to Executive and finally Senior Executive.
