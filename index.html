I am a PhD candidate at the Humanoid Robots Lab (University of Bonn) and a member of the PhenoRob Cluster of Excellence. My research focuses on active perception, semantic mapping, and uncertainty-aware AI for robotics, with a emphasis on agricultural manipulation and scene understanding under perceptual uncertainty.
